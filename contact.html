Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a tournament?

Go to the Tournaments page, select a tournament you want to join, and click the "Register" button. You'll need to complete the payment process to secure your spot.

When will I receive my prize money?

Prize money is typically processed within 24-48 hours after tournament completion. You'll receive a confirmation email once the payment has been initiated.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular digital wallets through our secure payment gateway.
